About Us

Fitzpatrick Basketball is a community run by past and present collegiate basketball players.

Goals

Fitzpatrick Basketball strives for goals that satisfy the players. Our main goal is to improve each player that comes into our program; especially skill work they want to get better in. Our mission is to develop good players into great players, and great players into phenominal ones! This can all happen at Fitzpatrick Basketball.

Locations

Fitzpatrick Basketball draws from a wide variety of sububran and urban kids. We have camps and clinics at local suburban high schools such as; Academy of Notre Dame, Cardinal O'Hara, and Malvern Prepatory High School. We also are located in the city running workouts at Saint Joseph's University and University of Pennsylvania.

Ellen Fitzpatrick is a well known star in the Philadelphia area. Scoring 994 points at Saint Joseph's, she was the point guard for a NCAA sweet 16 team. Ellen is not only in the Saint Joseph's University Hall of Fame, but also the Philadelphia Big 5. Being only 5'5", Ellen had to work extra hard on her skills to be the player that she had become. With that being said, Ellen can help you be the best player you will ever be.
