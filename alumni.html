Ciara Strode lead the Hawks of Saint Joseph's University in scoring in the 2014-2015 season. Her quickness and high basketball IQ helps her in her successes.

Natasha Cloud scored 1,000 points at Saint Joseph's. Her record of assists in one season at Saint Joe's is from her experience at Fitzpatrick Basketball. The spring of 2015, Natasha was drafted 15th overall in the WNBA.

Kathleen Fitzpatrick is a current starter at Saint Joseph's. She plays a solid amount of minutes playing against the best teams in the country. Kathleen is a consistent 3 point shooter learning her shooting ability from her years at Fitzpatrick Basketball. She is currently still in the Fitzpatrick program.
